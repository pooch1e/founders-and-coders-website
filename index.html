Why me?

I became interested in coding through university when I was studying fine art. One of my peers made an insane work that was an interactive television connected to a kinect, that melted your body into pixels when you stood in front of it (using processing). Since then, I've tried many times (and failed lots too) to pick up the skills, however, it has never super duper stuck. Everytime I try, I do learn a little bit more!

I wanna do x

Most of all, I do have a massive interest in the ethics behind tech. I think we have a responsiblity as practitioners to make considered and ethical choices about how we are using, creating and disseminating technology. The big watch word at the moment is AI. I'm super concerned that the bulk of research in this area is being driven by profit-driven corporations, which I do not think is a net positive for the world. I want to be able to understand how AI and software development works so I can better educate and support my peers and community in the future.
